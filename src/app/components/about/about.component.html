    Frequently Asked Questions


    What do you shoot?

    Who is your typical customer?
    My core customers are families, individuals, and event planners that are hosting parties, large or small, and celebrations. I especially love shooting engagements and baby showers/gender reveals because there's generally a certain element of surprise that makes for wonderful facial expressions. And most people these days do these events at nice locations, so it makes the job that much easier.
